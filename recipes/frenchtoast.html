Fluffy French Toast

Description

A recipe for fluffy french toast using flour. Source

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 12 thick slices bread

Steps

  1. Measure flour into large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in milk. Then, whisk in eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Soak bread slices in milk mixture until saturated.
  4. Working in batches, cook bread on preheated griddle or pan until golden brown on each side. Serve hot.
