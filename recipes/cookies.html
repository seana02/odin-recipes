Cookies

Description

Triple chocolate cookies that are crispy on the outside, chewy on the inside. Source

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup milk chocolate chips
  • 1 (3 oz) bar dark chocolate cut into chunks
  • 3 tablespoons white chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Combine brown sugar, butter, white sugar, salt in large bowl and beat with electric mixer until a creamy, deep brown mixture forms.
  2. Add egg and vanilla, beat until mixture lightens and becomes smooth, 10 to 15 seconds.
  3. Mix flour and baking soda in a separate bowl. Slowly add wet ingredients until mostly incorporated, but some white traces of flour remain.
  4. Fold in milk chocolate, dark chocolate, white chocolate using a spatula. Cover the dough and refrigerate for at least 30 min, up to 48 hours.
  5. Preheat oven to 350 F (175 C) when ready to bake.
  6. Divide cold dough into 2 1/4-oz portions and place on cookie sheet
  7. Bake in preheated oven until edges start to appear golden brown and crispy, 10-12 min. The centers will not look fully done.
  8. Cool until cookies are set, about 30 min.
